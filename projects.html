Perry's Projects

Perry's most intensive project was the construction of an AI player for Chess using Java. This project is split into two versions. The first is a player versus player version where two human opponents can play a game together. The second is an Alpha/Beta player that plays against the human. It possesses both minimax and evaluation functions that help it predict favorable moves.

This landing page also serves as another of Perry's projects, created to showcase his skills and experiences.
