Perry's Education

Perry was an undergraduate student of Skidmore College from September 2019 to May 2023. He had an English and Computer Science double major, and thus received a BA degree in the majors. Having graduated with a 3.7 GPA, he also held the honor of being a Cum Laude graduate.

Additionally, Perry also became a member of the Skidmore College Honors Forum. He attained the necessary Honors Forum credit and completed an Honors Forum project to become an Honors Forum graduate.
